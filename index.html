Our Name

Avir Toya means "continuous flowing fresh water" — a symbol of purity, flow, and life. It represents our commitment to crafting beverages that embody these qualities.

Our Vision

At Avir Toya, we believe that drinks are more than just beverages. They are crafted experiences, designed to refresh and inspire. Our philosophy is simple: to create drinks that flow seamlessly into your life, bringing moments of calm and joy.
